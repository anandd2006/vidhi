How I work

The process behind the magic

Same backbone for 3D and 2D: understand the goal, explore loosely, commit with care, then ship files you can actually use.

01
Ideate & research
References, mood, and goals — whether it's a brief for a flyer or blockout for a 3D scene, the idea comes first.
02
Sketch & blockout
Thumbnails, layouts, or greybox geometry to lock composition and readability before pushing detail.
03
Craft & texture
Refined lines, UVs, materials, and colour — iterating until the piece feels balanced on screen or in print.
04
Export & deliver
Clean file delivery, clear naming, and formats ready for web, print, or engine — plus notes for the next round.
